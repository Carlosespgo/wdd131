Bogota, Colombia

Bogotá, Colombia's vibrant capital, is a city that captivates with its rich history, cultural diversity, and pulsating modernity. Located in the heart of the Colombian Andes, this metropolis of more than 7 million inhabitants stands as the political, economic, and cultural center of the country. Situated at 2,640 meters above sea level, the city enjoys a spring-like climate throughout the year, making it unique among the world's major capitals.
